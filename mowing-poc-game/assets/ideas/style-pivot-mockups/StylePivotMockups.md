## Directions

### Flat Indie
- Goal: remove brushy texture and baked gradients in favor of cleaner color blocking, mild cel shading, and light outline accents.
- Read: strongest candidate so far for a classic 2D indie look while keeping the scene calm and readable.

### Retro Lite
- Goal: test a simplified limited-palette direction without going full pixel art.
- Read: useful as a boundary check, but it still feels softer and less decisive than the other two directions.

## Takeaways
- The painterly softness can be removed without losing map readability if prompts explicitly preserve layout and obstacle positions.
- `Flat Indie` keeps the friendliest balance between readability and marketable style.
- `Bold Cartoon` is the clearest departure from the current look and worth exploring if the game should feel more playful.
- `Retro Lite` does not currently buy enough stylistic identity to justify its reduced richness.

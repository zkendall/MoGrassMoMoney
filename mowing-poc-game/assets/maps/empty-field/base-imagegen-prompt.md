# Empty Field Base Art Prompt

Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: gameplay background for the `empty_field` mowing map
Primary request: top-down flat-indie empty field for mowing experimentation
Scene/background: a wide open grass field with no props, no house, no driveway, and no decorative landmarks
Subject: a single broad mowable rectangle intended for testing grass visuals and mower mechanics
Style/medium: top-down 2D flat-color indie game art with an even grass plane, minimal texture, and no scene clutter
Composition/framing: exact gameplay framing for a 960x640 map, straight top-down camera, no perspective tilt
Lighting/mood: bright neutral daytime, simple soft shading only, readable and uncluttered
Color palette: fresh readable green grass with restrained saturation
Materials/textures: flat lawn surface with little to no texture; avoid mottling or dappled shading
Quality: high
Constraints: no mower, no characters, no text, no logos, no watermark, no obstacles, no buildings, no paths
Avoid: painterly texture, noisy grass detail, isometric angle, dramatic lighting, props, clutter

Layout notes from the gameplay guide:
- Full mowable field is a large rectangle roughly spanning `(60, 40)` to `(900, 600)`.
- Keep the field visually plain so grass art and mowing mechanics are easy to judge in isolation.
